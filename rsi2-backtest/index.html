About This Backtest

Strategy: Buy when price pulls back to RSI(2) < 10 during an uptrend (above 200-SMA), exit when price recovers above 5-SMA.
Execution: All entries and exits at next-day open (no lookahead bias).
Universe: All 1384 tickers in price_cache with 250+ bars of history.
Scientist Mode Verified: 15/15 spot checks passed, 0 lookahead violations, 0 overlapping trades.
Key Finding: Strategy works best on trending, liquid assets (PF 2-4x). Fails on frontier markets, commodities without persistent uptrends, and assets with structural decline. Min 20 trades required for meaningful stats (marked with * if fewer).

About This Dashboard
What It Does
Measures cosine distance between consecutive regime fingerprints and uses CUSUM changepoint detection to identify regime transitions. Tracks drift rate, pole flip analysis, and similarity to known historical macro windows (e.g. 2008 crisis, 2020 COVID).
Today's Reading
Today's regime analysis shows a market environment most similar to the 2013 taper tantrum period (32.7% similarity), with moderate drift at 1.97 normalized units in the NORMAL tier. The current market fingerprint is notably distant from both the 2008 financial crisis (21.3% similarity) and the recent AI bull regime (8.4% similarity), suggesting we're not in a classic risk-off deleveraging scenario or a pure growth-driven rally. The 2020 COVID crash regime shows minimal resemblance at just 0.6% similarity, indicating recovery-phase dynamics are not dominant. This pattern points toward a market characterized by external shock sensitivity and policy uncertainty rather than systematic risk repricing or momentum-driven strength.

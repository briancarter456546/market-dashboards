What It Does

Today's Reading
Today's regime analysis shows the market is operating in an ELEVATED drift state with a normalized drift rate of 2.75, suggesting meaningful structural shifts in market behavior. Overall, the data points to a market transitioning within accommodative conditions rather than entering a sharp regime break, though the elevated drift metric warrants continued monitoring for changepoint signals.

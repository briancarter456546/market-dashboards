About This Dashboard
What It Does
Visualizes the ~20 ETF poles (out of 43 total) that passed reliability testing via RSI(2) mean-reversion backtest with Profit Factor >= 1.5. Each pole is a Factor-Mimicking Portfolio (equal-weighted top 5 ETF constituents). Heatmap cards show 1W/1M/3M returns color-coded by momentum. Performance table includes 6M and YTD returns plus backtest trust scores (PF, win rate) and SPY beta. Poles excluded from regression (US Large Cap Core, US Dollar, Cash, Dow, S&P Variants) are removed.
For educational and informational purposes only. Not financial advice. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
